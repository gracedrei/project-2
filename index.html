Capital Ale House

Capital Ale House is Virginia’s Ale House. We are locally owned and serve only the best craft beer from around the world, across the USA, and local Virginia brews. We strive to procure other local products when possible. Please visit any of our five locations near you and enjoy a great meal, delicious craft beer, and our Virginia hospitality.
